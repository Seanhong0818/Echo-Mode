## 🧩 Forking Policy

You are **welcome** to fork, remix, or build upon Echo Mode SDK, provided that:

- You retain the original attribution (see `Echo_Attribution_Template.md`)
- You clearly indicate **modifications** or changes to behavior
- You do **not claim Echo Mode as your original invention**
- You include the Echo Open License v1.2 in any redistributed version
